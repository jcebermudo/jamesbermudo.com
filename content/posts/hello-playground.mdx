I've been wanting a place to think out loud for a while.

Not a polished portfolio. Not a Twitter thread. Something more like a lab notebook — a place where half-formed ideas can sit next to finished ones, where I can work through something publicly and see where it leads.

So here it is: **The Playground**.

## What this is

A research log. I'll write about things I'm actively learning or building:

- **AI & LLMs** — mechanistic interpretability, quantization, inference optimization, scaling laws, and the occasional paper breakdown
- **Math** — things I find beautiful or useful: linear algebra, probability, information theory
- **Experiments** — small projects, ablations, results from things I've tried

Some posts will be long technical reports. Others will just be a few paragraphs and a code snippet. The format will follow the idea.

## Why public?

Writing forces clarity. If I can't explain it plainly, I don't understand it well enough yet.

And occasionally, someone finds it useful. That's enough.
